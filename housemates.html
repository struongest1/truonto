Jimmy

Jimmy is a recent MSc. grad from the U of T. Jimmy has a green thumb and a 'thing' for jungle plants, compost and edible vegetables. A chemist by training, he brings his passion for mixing and concocting into the home by way of his recipes, odd projects and gardening. Dano

Dano recently graduated with a MSc. in Neurophysiology from the U of T and has gotten himself a job in tech. He's building a desktop application for an EEG device company that will let researchers collect, analyze, and visualize EEG data from a wireless EEG headband. Generally, the intersection between neuroscience and tech is where Dano wants to be.

Dano enjoys brewing beer, skiing, hiking and playing the occasional video game (curr: Legend of Zelda Breath of the Wild). He also sings and plays ukulele and guitar, sometimes at odd hours of the day. Enjoys gardening but does not have a green thumb. Cycles everywhere. Dano makes his presence felt in the house, but hopes that it is bouyant and joyful.
