A verdant oasis

We have a backyard with an extensive garden. Grapes, herbs, tomatoes, and raspberries all make an appearance during the summer and fall. We often use our sheltered backyard patio for meals, work, and entertaining guests.

Unintentionally boho-chic

Cobbled together from Vietnamese family heirlooms, poor-student-improvisations, and live plants, our interiors may be slightly cluttered, but give off a redeemably comfortable and lived-in vibe.

Gastronomic

We like nice food and drinking coffee together in the morning. Fancy mac and cheese is a common late night occurence. Sometimes we turn our house into a coffee shop and make drinks for our friends.
